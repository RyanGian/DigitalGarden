### 🍃 About This Garden

Throughout my career as a software engineer, I’ve occasionally found myself forgetting things due to the overwhelming volume of information I need to manage. 

This Digital Garden was made in hopes to help me retain information and grow with new concepts. It also allows me to reflect along the way, refining and strengthening my knowledge overtime. This documentation was heavily inspired by another fellow software engineer who at some point helped me on this journey of learning. 

Some notes in this garden may still be unfinished, but like in any garden, it takes time for young ideas to grow and flourish. I will always continue to add and grow to this garden. 

### 🗺️ Navigating the Garden

 - **Graph View**: Each topic, and concept are represented via nodes. Click on the graph icon on the Graph View to see a visualization of all connected nodes.
 
- **Search**: To find specific topics, use the search bar (`Ctrl + K`).

- **Tags**: Each note may have tags to help you discover related content.

In some notes, there will be popover previews of certain concepts. This helps my existing knowledge carry over into future concepts, making it easier to build on what I already know.
